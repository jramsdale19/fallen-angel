Chapter 0: 



I knew the end was near. 
The dream was coming to an end. 
The light flooding my eyes from coming from the entrance of the cave. I had been hiding in the darkness for so long, time unaware of my rest. 
I wanted to crawl deeper into the cave, into the obscurity of ignorance. 
My time had come. 
As the world began to vibrate and spin I knew. 
Time was a never ending churn, an energy beyond human. 
I had been found and now thrust into the light.
